The goal of the renovation is to change the room into space that combine simplicity and functionality. The room is supposed to serve as office as well as relax room.

1. Emptying the room from unnecessary to renovation items
    Furniture and other items are carried out to different room.
2. Preparing walls, ceiling and floor
    Walls and ceiling are cleaned up, defects are fixed. Floor is removed.
3. Laying a new floor
    The new flooring is being installed on the prepared surface.
4. Painting
    Walls and ceiling are being painted.
5. Lightning replacement
    The existing lightning is being replaced with minimalist LED lamps. Additionally, a lamp is being installed in the desk area.
6. Furnishing the room
    Modular furniture is being introduced and strategically positioned to optimize functional space while preserving a clean, modern aesthetic.
